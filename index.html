The Crystal Collecter

The Rules


At the start of the game you will be given a random number.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total output.

To win the game, match your total output to the random number, you lose the game if your total output goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

The values of each crystal will charge at the start of a new game.
